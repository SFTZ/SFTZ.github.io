SFTZ & FBOL COINS

SFTZ and FBOL is a peer-to-peer Internet currencies based on hybrid Proof-of-Work / Proof-of-Stake block generation methods that enables instant, near-zero cost payments to anyone in the world. This is an open source, global payment networks that is fully decentralized and released under the MIT/X11 license which gives you the power to run, modify, and copy the software and to distribute, at your option, modified copies of the software. The software is released in a transparent process that allows for independent verification of binaries and their corresponding source code.

PRIVACY AND SECURITY

Protect your financial information. Your transactions are private and secure. Every wallet allows complete privacy using the 5 series account number. Privacy features support anonymous transactions without the need of master nodes or mixing services when using the 5 series address in the wallet. SFTZ and FBOL coins transactions don’t require you to give up any secret information. Instead, they use two keys: a public key, and a private one. Anyone can see the public key (which is your address), but your private key is secret. When you send a SFTZ or FBOL Coin, you ‘sign’ the transaction by combining your public and private keys together and applying a mathematical function to them. This creates a certificate that proves the transaction came from you. If you don’t publish your private key for everyone to see, you’re safe.

SPEED

The SFTZ and FBOL blockchains are capable of handling higher transaction volume than its counterpart - Bitcoin. Due to more frequent block generation, the network supports more transactions without a need to modify the software in the future. As a result, merchants get faster confirmation times, while still having ability to wait for more confirmations when selling bigger ticket items.
